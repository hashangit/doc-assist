# Document Selector

Guide for choosing the right Product Management or Marketing document.

## Quick Decision Tree

```
START: What are you trying to do?

├── Define long-term direction or goals?
│   └──→ pm-strategy-docs
│       ├── Vision (3-5 year direction)
│       ├── Strategy (How to win)
│       ├── Roadmap (Timeline/priorities)
│       └── OKRs (Measurable goals)
│
├── Specify what to build or for whom?
│   └──→ pm-discovery-docs
│       ├── PRD (Feature specifications)
│       ├── User Stories (Requirements)
│       ├── JTBD (Customer motivations)
│       ├── Personas (User archetypes)
│       └── Journey Map (Experience mapping)
│
├── Research market or validate ideas?
│   └──→ pm-research-docs
│       ├── Market Research (Competitive/size/trends)
│       ├── User Research (Interviews/surveys)
│       └── Opportunity Assessment (Business case)
│
├── Define positioning or messaging?
│   └──→ marketing-positioning-docs
│       ├── Positioning Statement (Market position)
│       ├── Messaging Framework (Key messages)
│       ├── Value Prop Canvas (Pains/gains mapping)
│       └── Brand Guidelines (Voice/visual identity)
│
├── Plan content or campaigns?
│   └──→ marketing-content-docs
│       ├── Content Strategy (Pillars/calendar)
│       ├── Campaign Brief (Marketing campaign)
│       ├── Competitor Battlecard (Sales enablement)
│       └── Case Study Brief (Customer story)
│
├── Launch product or enable sales?
│   └──→ marketing-launch-docs
│       ├── GTM Strategy (Launch plan)
│       ├── Launch Checklist (Readiness)
│       ├── Product Datasheet (Feature summary)
│       └── Sales Enablement Kit (Pitch/demo/FAQ)
│
└── Create cross-functional documents?
    └──→ bridge-docs
        ├── Product Brief (Feature summary)
        ├── Release Notes (Shipped value)
        ├── FAQ/Help Center (User education)
        └── Demo Script (Product demos)
```

## By Situation

### Starting a New Product/Feature

| Stage | Document | Why |
|-------|----------|-----|
| Ideation | Market Research | Validate market opportunity |
| Definition | Opportunity Assessment | Build business case |
| Planning | Product Brief | Align stakeholders |
| Discovery | User Personas | Understand target users |
| Discovery | JTBD | Understand motivations |
| Specification | PRD | Detail requirements |
| Planning | Product Roadmap | Set timeline |

### Preparing for Launch

| Timing | Document | Why |
|--------|----------|-----|
| 3-6 months before | GTM Strategy | Plan the launch |
| 3-6 months before | Positioning Statement | Define market position |
| 2-3 months before | Messaging Framework | Create key messages |
| 2-3 months before | Product Datasheet | Summarize for sales |
| 1-2 months before | Sales Enablement Kit | Equip sales team |
| 1-2 months before | Competitor Battlecard | Handle objections |
| 2-4 weeks before | Launch Checklist | Ensure readiness |
| Launch day | Release Notes | Announce to users |
| Post-launch | Case Study Brief | Capture success stories |

### Working with Customers

| Need | Document | Why |
|------|----------|-----|
| Understand users | User Personas | Create archetypes |
| Map experience | Customer Journey Map | Identify pain points |
| Understand motivation | JTBD | Find jobs to solve |
| Research needs | User Research Report | Share findings |
| Tell success story | Case Study Brief | Plan case study |
| Educate users | FAQ/Help Center | Self-service support |
| Demo product | Demo Script | Guide demonstration |

### Strategic Planning

| Need | Document | Why |
|------|----------|-----|
| Set direction | Product Vision | Long-term aspiration |
| Plan how to win | Product Strategy | Competitive approach |
| Set timeline | Product Roadmap | Feature priorities |
| Measure success | OKRs | Quarterly goals |
| Analyze market | Market Research | Competitive landscape |
| Assess opportunity | Opportunity Assessment | Investment decision |
