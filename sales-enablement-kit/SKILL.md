## 2. Demo Script

### Pre-Demo Checklist
- [ ] Environment ready
- [ ] Test data loaded
- [ ] Key workflows practiced
- [ ] Backup screenshots ready

### Demo Flow

#### Opening (2 min)
"Thanks for your time today. Before we dive in, can you tell me a bit about what you're hoping to see?"

[Listening prompts:]
- What's your current process for [X]?
- What's working well? What's not?
- Who else is involved in this decision?

#### Setup (1 min)
"Based on what you've shared, I'll focus on [specific features]. Let me show you how [Product] helps with [their pain point]."

#### Core Demo (10-15 min)

**Scenario 1: [Common use case]**
1. Start at: [Screen/URL]
2. Action: [What to click/show]
3. Say: "[Narration - connect to their pain]"
4. Highlight: [Key feature]
5. Value: "[Quantifiable benefit]"

**Scenario 2: [Second use case]**
[Same structure]

**Scenario 3: [Differentiator]**
[Same structure]

#### Closing (3 min)
"Based on what you've seen, what questions do you have?"

[Transition to next steps based on interest]

### Demo Do's and Don'ts
**Do:**
- Tailor to their specific needs
- Pause for questions
- Connect features to their pain points

**Don't:**
- Show every feature
- Rush through
- Get stuck on technical issues

## 3. Objection Handling Guide

| Objection | Response | Pivot |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| "It's too expensive" | "I understand budget is a consideration. Let's look at the ROI - our customers typically see [X] results in [Y] time. What would it mean for your business if you could [achieve outcome]?" | "Many of our customers had similar concerns. Can I share how [similar customer] justified the investment?" |
| "We're already using [competitor]" | "What's working well with [competitor]? [Listen] Many customers who switched told us they appreciated [our differentiator]. Would it be helpful to see how we compare on [their priority]?" | "Would you be open to a trial to see the difference firsthand?" |
| "We need to think about it" | "Of course. What specific concerns do you have? Is there additional information that would help?" | "Who else needs to be involved in this decision? Would a call with them be helpful?" |
| "Not the right time" | "I understand timing matters. What would need to happen for this to become a priority?" | "Would it make sense to schedule a follow-up for [specific timeframe]?" |
| "We need more features" | "Which specific features are you looking for? [Listen] Let me show you how we handle that today..." | "That's on our roadmap for [timeframe]. Would you like me to keep you updated?" |
