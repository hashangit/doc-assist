# Product Vision

Define the long-term direction for your product (3-5 year horizon).

## Document Creation Workflow

### Step 1: Setup Directory Structure
Create the doc-assist folder structure in the project root if it doesn't exist:
```
doc-assist/
├── project-management/
├── product-management/
├── marketing/
└── bridge/
```

### Step 2: Generate Document
### Step 3: Preview for User
Display the complete document content to the user in a code block for review.

### Step 4: Confirm and Save
Ask the user: "Does this document meet your expectations? Should I save it?"

**If confirmed:**
- Save to `doc-assist/product-management/product-vision.md`
- Confirm the save location with the user

## Template

```markdown
# Product Vision: [Product Name]

## Vision Statement
[One compelling sentence describing the future state]

## The World Today
[Current state of the market/problem space - 2-3 paragraphs]

## The Future We See
[The world after your product succeeds - 2-3 paragraphs]

## Our Product's Role
[How your product enables that future - 2-3 paragraphs]

## Strategic Pillars
1. **[Pillar 1]:** [Description]
2. **[Pillar 2]:** [Description]
3. **[Pillar 3]:** [Description]

## Target Market
- **Primary:** [Who]
- **Secondary:** [Who]
- **Market Size:** [$X B/TAM]

## Success Measures (3-5 Years)
| Metric | Current | Target | Why It Matters |
|--------|---------|--------|----------------|
| [Metric] | [Value] | [Value] | [Reason] |

## Competitive Differentiation
[What makes us different and defensible]

## Risks to Vision
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| [Risk] | [H/M/L] | [Strategy] |

## Key Dependencies
- [Dependency 1]
- [Dependency 2]

## Document History
| Version | Date | Author | Changes |
|---------|------|--------|---------|
| 1.0 | [Date] | [Name] | Initial |
```
